Metropolitan Business Center

Status: In Progress - 65% Complete

A state-of-the-art commercial building that sets new standards for sustainable business environments in Metro Manila's rapidly growing business district. This flagship project showcases DDC Homes' expertise in large-scale commercial construction and commitment to environmental responsibility.

Project Overview

The Metropolitan Business Center represents the future of commercial architecture, combining cutting-edge design with sustainable construction practices. This 12-story tower will house multiple businesses, retail spaces, and modern office environments designed to foster productivity and collaboration.

Advanced Features & Technology

  • Green Roof System: Living roof with native plants for natural insulation and rainwater management
  • Smart HVAC Controls: AI-powered climate control reducing energy consumption by 40%
  • Triple-Glazed Glass Facade: Maximum natural light with superior thermal performance
  • Integrated Security Suite: Biometric access, 24/7 monitoring, and emergency response systems
  • Flexible Workspace Design: Modular layouts adaptable to various business needs
  • High-Speed Fiber Infrastructure: Future-ready connectivity throughout the building
  • Electric Vehicle Charging: 50 dedicated EV charging stations in the parking facility
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Sustainable water management system

  • Water-efficient fixtures reducing consumption by 35%
