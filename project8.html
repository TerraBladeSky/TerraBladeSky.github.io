Commercial Building

Status: In Progress

Modern commercial building currently under construction in the heart of Valenzuela's growing business district. This state-of-the-art facility represents the future of sustainable commercial architecture, combining cutting-edge design with environmentally conscious construction practices.

DDC Homes is proud to be at the forefront of green building technology with this landmark project. The building is designed to achieve LEED Gold certification and will serve as a model for sustainable commercial construction in the Philippines.

Key Features & Innovations

  • Green roof installation - Living roof system that reduces energy costs and provides natural insulation
  • High-efficiency HVAC systems - Variable refrigerant flow technology with smart climate controls
  • Energy-efficient glass facade - Triple-pane low-E glass with automated shading systems
  • Advanced security systems - Biometric access controls and 24/7 monitoring capabilities
  • Collaborative workspace design - Flexible floor plans with movable walls and modular furniture systems
    • Sustainable Design

    Our green roof system will reduce the building's carbon footprint by 30% while providing natural cooling and stormwater management.

    Modern Interiors

    Flexible workspace design accommodates various business needs with open collaborative areas and private meeting spaces.

    Smart Technology

    Integrated building management system controls lighting, climate, and security for optimal efficiency and user comfort.
