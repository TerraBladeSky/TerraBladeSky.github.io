Lakeside Custom Home

Waterfront luxury residence with sustainable design and panoramic lake views.

Project Overview

This stunning 6,500 square foot lakeside residence represents the pinnacle of luxury living combined with environmental consciousness. Designed for a discerning family who values both elegance and sustainability, this custom home seamlessly blends modern architecture with natural surroundings.

Our team collaborated closely with the clients for over 18 months to create their dream home, incorporating cutting-edge technology and premium materials throughout. Every detail was carefully considered to maximize both comfort and energy efficiency.

Location: Crystal Lake

Size: 6,500 sq ft

Year: 2021

Timeline: 14 months

Budget: $2.8M

Special Features & Amenities

  • Geothermal heating system - Reduces energy costs by 60% while providing consistent comfort
  • Infinity edge pool - Seamlessly blends with the lake horizon for breathtaking views
  • Custom millwork throughout - Hand-crafted cabinetry and built-ins using locally sourced hardwoods
  • Home automation system - Smart controls for lighting, climate, security, and entertainment
  • Private boat dock - 60-foot pier with boat lift and watercraft storage
  • Floor-to-ceiling windows - Maximize natural light and lake views from every room
  • Gourmet chef's kitchen - Professional-grade appliances and granite waterfall island
  • Master suite sanctuary - Private balcony, spa bathroom, and walk-in closet
  • Wine cellar - Climate-controlled storage for 500+ bottles
  • Guest house - Separate 800 sq ft accommodation for visitors

    Sustainable Design Elements

    Environmental stewardship was a core priority in this project. The home achieves a 40% reduction in energy consumption compared to similar-sized conventional homes through innovative design and technology.

    Green Technologies:
  • Geothermal heating and cooling system
  • Solar panel array (25kW capacity)
  • Rainwater harvesting system
  • LED lighting throughout
  • High-performance insulation and windows
  • Native landscaping requiring minimal irrigation

    • Construction Excellence

    This project showcased DDC Homes' expertise in complex custom construction. Working on a sloped lakefront lot presented unique challenges that our team overcame with innovative engineering solutions.

    Technical Highlights:
  • Engineered foundation system for sloped terrain
  • Structural steel frame for large open spaces
  • Waterproofing systems for lakefront conditions
  • Integration of smart home technologies
  • Custom architectural millwork and finishes
