Historic Downtown Residential Renovation

A masterful blend of heritage preservation and modern luxury

The Challenge

This beautiful 1920s heritage home in the downtown district had fallen into disrepair after decades of neglect. The owners wanted to restore its original grandeur while incorporating modern conveniences for contemporary living. The challenge was to maintain the home's historic character while meeting current building codes and energy efficiency standards.

Our Solution

Our team developed a comprehensive renovation plan that respected the home's architectural heritage while seamlessly integrating 21st-century amenities. Every detail was carefully planned to ensure historical accuracy while providing the comfort and functionality today's families demand.

The home before renovation began

Custom kitchen with period-appropriate details

Project Highlights

  • Full structural reinforcement - Updated foundation and load-bearing elements to modern standards
  • Period-accurate restoration work - Restored original hardwood floors, crown molding, and decorative plasterwork
  • Smart home technology integration - Invisible wiring for modern conveniences without compromising aesthetics
  • Energy-efficient window replacement - Custom-made windows matching original profiles with modern insulation
  • Custom kitchen and bath designs - Handcrafted cabinetry and vintage-inspired fixtures
  • HVAC system upgrade - Concealed ductwork maintains period appearance
  • Electrical system modernization - Complete rewiring hidden within existing walls
