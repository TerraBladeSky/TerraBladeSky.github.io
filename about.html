Building Dreams Since 1998

For over 25 years, DDC Homes has been transforming visions into reality, one project at a time. From humble beginnings as a family carpentry business to becoming Valenzuela's premier custom home builder, our journey is built on trust, quality, and unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Story

From Carpenter to Contractor

Founded in 1998 by During Dela Cruz, DDC Homes began as a one-man carpentry service operating from a small workshop in Valenzuela. Armed with traditional craftsmanship skills passed down through generations and an unwavering commitment to quality, During started by taking on small repair jobs and custom furniture pieces for neighbors.

What set us apart from the beginning was our attention to detail and our belief that every project, no matter how small, deserved the same level of care and precision. Word spread quickly through the community about the young carpenter who treated every home as if it were his own.

By 2000, we had grown from a solo operation to a small team of skilled craftsmen, each sharing the same passion for excellence that During embodied. This growth wasn't just about taking on more projects—it was about building a reputation that would become the foundation of everything we do today.

Our Evolution

1998-2005: Roofing Specialists

We started as roofing experts, mastering weather-resistant installations and repairs across Valenzuela. Our signature double-layer waterproofing technique became our trademark, protecting homes through countless storms and monsoons. During this period, we completed over 300 roofing projects, earning recognition for our innovative approaches to Philippine weather challenges.

2006-2010: Home Renovations Era

Expanding into full-home renovations, we transformed outdated spaces into modern living areas. Our "Whole-House Revival" approach won the 2009 Metro Manila Renovator's Award. We pioneered the concept of sustainable renovation, incorporating eco-friendly materials and energy-efficient designs that reduced utility costs for homeowners by an average of 30%.

2011-2014: Apartment Construction

We entered multi-unit development, constructing 12 apartment complexes featuring innovative space-saving designs. Our Vista Heights project housed 200 families with green building techniques, including rainwater harvesting systems and solar panel installations. This era established our reputation for creating affordable, sustainable housing solutions.

2015-Present: Custom Home Masters

Since 2015, we've specialized in bespoke luxury homes. Each custom build features architectural uniqueness and smart-home integration, with over 50 dream homes completed to date. Our projects now include features like home automation systems, renewable energy integration, and designs that seamlessly blend indoor and outdoor living—perfect for the Philippine climate.
