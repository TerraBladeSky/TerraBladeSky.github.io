About the Business

Our Story

Founded in 1998 by During Dela Cruz, our construction business began as a small carpentry service...

Our Evolution

1998-2005: Roofing Specialists

We started as roofing experts, mastering weather-resistant installations and repairs across Valenzuela. Our signature double-layer waterproofing technique became our trademark.

2006-2010: Home Renovations Era

Expanding into full-home renovations, we transformed outdated spaces into modern living areas. Our "Whole-House Revival" approach won the 2009 Metro Manila Renovator's Award.

2011-2014: Apartment Construction

We entered multi-unit development, constructing 12 apartment complexes featuring innovative space-saving designs. Our Vista Heights project housed 200 families with green building techniques.

2015-Present: Custom Home Masters

Since 2015, we've specialized in bespoke luxury homes. Each custom build features architectural uniqueness and smart-home integration, with over 50 dream homes completed to date.
