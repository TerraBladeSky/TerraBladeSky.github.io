Modern Farmhouse Estate

A stunning blend of contemporary design and rustic charm, this custom-built farmhouse represents the perfect harmony between modern luxury and timeless country elegance.

Project Overview

This breathtaking modern farmhouse was meticulously crafted on a sprawling 5-acre property in the heart of the countryside. Our team at DDC Homes transformed the client's vision of a sophisticated yet welcoming family retreat into reality, incorporating sustainable building practices with premium materials throughout.

The design seamlessly blends traditional farmhouse aesthetics with contemporary functionality, featuring clean lines, expansive windows, and carefully curated rustic elements. Every detail was thoughtfully planned to create a home that feels both grand and intimate, perfect for both everyday living and entertaining.

Project Details

Size: 4,200 sq ft

Duration: 10 months

Year: 2018

Lot Size: 5 acres

Bedrooms: 4

Bathrooms: 3.5

Style: Modern Farmhouse

Featured Highlights

Gourmet Chef's Kitchen

The heart of the home features professional-grade appliances, custom cabinetry with soft-close drawers, and a stunning quartz waterfall island that serves as both workspace and gathering spot for family meals.

Expansive Wraparound Porch

Enjoy morning coffee or evening sunsets on this beautiful covered porch that wraps around three sides of the home, offering panoramic views of the meticulously landscaped grounds.

Charming Guest Cottage

A separate 800 sq ft guest cottage provides privacy for visitors while maintaining the same design aesthetic as the main house, complete with kitchenette and full bathroom.

Luxurious Master Suite

The master bedroom features vaulted ceilings with exposed beams, a cozy sitting area by the fireplace, and French doors opening to a private deck overlooking the property.

    Organic Landscape Design

    The property features thoughtfully designed outdoor spaces that complement the farmhouse aesthetic while providing year-round beauty and functionality. Our landscape design incorporates native plants, sustainable irrigation, and carefully planned outdoor living areas.

    Highlights include a vegetable garden with raised beds, fruit tree orchard, wildflower meadow, and stone pathways connecting various outdoor spaces. The design prioritizes low maintenance while maximizing the property's natural beauty.
